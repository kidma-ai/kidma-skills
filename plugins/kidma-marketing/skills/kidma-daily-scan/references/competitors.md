## Global category (lessons + watch)
- **Khanmigo**: "only around 15% of students who have access to Khanmigo engage with it" (עובדה, Khan Academy official blog); 108M+ interactions since 2023; full redesign rolling out summer 2026; weak outside math; Hebrew unsupported.
- **eSelf → Kaltura**: acquired 5.11.2025, ~$27M total (עובדה, Ynetnews/SEC); was running national avatar-tutor pilot with מט"ח (10K students, 5/2025). (הסקה) Focus may shift to enterprise — watch whether the national education track slows.
- **Merlyn Mind → MYND.AI** (9/2025); founder Satya Nitta publicly called "chatbot can teach like a human" a deep misunderstanding of AI capabilities (11/2024).
- **LAUSD "Ed"/AllHere**: $6M contract, vendor collapse within months, Chapter 7, founder arrested for fraud 11/2024, FBI raid on LAUSD superintendent's home 2/2026 (עובדה, EdSurge/The 74/NYT). Lessons: student-data privacy is existential; over-promising collapses; human oversight essential.
- Code.org/CodeAI (150M+ students, no Hebrew found) · Experience AI (RPi+DeepMind, $10M Google.org, no Hebrew confirmed) — free/quality but English, no local accompaniment.
