## Quick Reference (always in context)

**Company:** Kidma Technologies and Education Ltd. (קידמה טכנולוגיות וחינוך בע״מ) | Israeli EdTech
**Positioning:** An Israeli EdTech platform for pedagogy and AI in schools, community centers (מתנ״סים), and teacher/principal training — "an AI system that teaches AI."
**Slogan:** מבינים. מנסים. יוצרים. (Understand. Try. Create.)
**Founders:** Adar Frenkel (Technology) & Almog Tayar (Pedagogy & Product)
**Mission:** Teach AI through AI — preparing students, teachers, and schools for the AI era
**Product:** Kidma Portal — LMS integrating ChatGPT, DALL·E, Gemini, Claude, Suno, Veo (and more leading world-class tools, not stripped-down educational versions) with pedagogical mediation
**Audiences:** students (elementary → high school), teaching staff, school management/principals, and community centers (מתנ״סים)
**Trust signals:** Ministry-of-Education-approved · Gefen-funded (מאושר משה״ח · ממומן גפ״ן) · gmul-recognized teacher training. Bootstrapped and profitable; strong retention — schools renew year after year.

### Key Programs

- **Student courses:** half-year track (~16 sessions) and annual track (~32 sessions), plus **Advanced B** (second year — projects + community impact). Current-year lessons (תשפ"ו): Advanced A (11) + Advanced B (7) + standalone workshops. All registered with the Ministry of Education and funded via Gefen (MoE catalog #s: 45986, 46920, 47732, 47742, 47746).
- **Teaching staff — "Or" program (תוכנית אור):** a **30-hour, gmul-recognized** professional-development course (MoE program **47746**, online). Built on the training triangle of **technology + pedagogy + psychology** — the same triangle that anchors teacher and principal/management education. "AI doesn't replace teachers — it frees them to do what only a human can."
- **Workshops:** Entrepreneurship (6 sessions), Webmaster AI / BASE44, AI video, Presentations, Financial education, Self-Learning (5 sessions), Intro to Practical AI (2 sessions)

### 2028 Vision

Flip the ratio from 1 teacher : 30 students → 30 AI pedagogical agents : 1 student. Multi-agent system, cognitive learning field, expand from AI subject to all subjects.
